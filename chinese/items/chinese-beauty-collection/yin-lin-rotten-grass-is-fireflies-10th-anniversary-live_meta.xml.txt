When recording the harmony, Hui Hui said that no matter how he tried to write, it didn’t quite fit, so I thought about trying it myself. The moment I opened my mouth, I seemed to recall how I sang the harmony of Rotten Grass 10 years ago, and the melody came out naturally.&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;div&gt;&lt;i&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;div&gt;&lt;i&gt;Speaking of which, Rotten Grass Is Fireflies was the first album I recorded in a recording studio. At that time, I was not used to being alone in front of a microphone in such a quiet, isolated room.&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;div&gt;&lt;i&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;div&gt;&lt;i&gt;But now, when facing many singers who are not familiar with the recording studio, I would tell them, it doesn’t matter, just sing more and you won’t be so scared once you get used to it.&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;div&gt;&lt;i&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;div&gt;&lt;i&gt;Ten years have passed, and I am about to release my fourth album and my first tour.
