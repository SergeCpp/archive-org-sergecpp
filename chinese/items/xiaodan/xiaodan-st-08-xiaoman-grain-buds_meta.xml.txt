When I heard "It's a fish leaping in the lotus pond, startling one tail, only you know the waves", I thought it should be a very lively summer feeling, with scattered sunlight shining through the bamboo leaves, sparkling streams and small fish jumping up from time to time, and the air is full of hot and humid smell. When listening to the music, there will be movements in my mind, and I finished the chorus part quickly following the rhythm. I did consider the main part for a long time. I wanted to step on the light sound of water drops and every drum beat, but I also wanted the movements to be slow to highlight the chorus. In the end, I didn't finish the chorus, but I just danced it like this.
